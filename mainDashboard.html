Need a Business Permit?

Requirements

Small/Local Businesses: Secure DTI registration before going to the barangay.

OR

For Businesses in Malls: Secure both DTI registration and lease contract before going to the barangay.

Business Permit Application

Walk-in: Proceed to the information desk for assistance. You are required to fill up a form for the processing of your Business Permit.

OR

Online: Click the button below for online application.

Claim Business Permit

Walk-in: After filling up necessary information, processing and claiming of documents are done the same day.

OR

Online: Visit the barangay office to claim your documents.
