How to apply for a business permit?

Step 1: Business Name Registration with the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI)

1.1. For small-scale and local businesses, owners are required to apply for business name registration
with the DTI and secure the necessary documents prior to proceeding to the barangay.

OR

1.2. For businesses operating within M Sta. Rosa and Robinsons Mall - Sta. Rosa, both the DTI
registration and the lease of contract must be secured and processed prior to proceeding to the barangay.

Step 2: Application for Business Permit from Barangay Tagapo

2.1. Walk-in applications for business permits are accepted. Applicants should proceed to the information
desk for guidance and assistance.

Please note that for walk-in applicants, the processing of document/s is completed on the same day of application.

OR

2.2. For online applications, applicants must complete the necessary information through the following link: (insert Google Forms link)

After submitting the online application, applicants are advised to visit the barangay office to claim the necessary document/s.
